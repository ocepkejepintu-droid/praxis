# autonovel

Source: hermes-atlas
Category: Core & Official
Author: NousResearch

Autonovel is an autonomous agent pipeline designed to transform a seed concept into a complete, publishable novel. It utilizes a multi-phase workflow that includes world-building, sequential drafting, and an adversarial revision loop involving dual-persona LLM reviewers. The system integrates mechanical slop detection and voice fingerprinting to ensure prose quality and narrative consistency across tens of thousands of words. Beyond text generation, the project automates typesetting in LaTeX, cover art creation via fal.ai, and multi-voice audiobook production using ElevenLabs. Highlights: Autonomous four-phase pipeline from world-building to final export; Adversarial revision loop with mechanical and LLM-based evaluation; Automated typesetting, cover illustration, and multi-voice audiobook generation Autonomous novel-writing pipeline generating 100k+ word manuscripts
